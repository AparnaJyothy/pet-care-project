About Us

Many people are afraid of adopting pets because they are worried about their well-being and illnesses. At Claws and Paws, we provide cats and dogs in good health for adoption. Before offering it for adoption, it is cared for and given vaccinations. We also offer the necessary toys, accessories, and wholesome food for the dogs and cats.

Why Adopt ?

  • Pets reduces our stress and provides lots of emotional support.
  • They strengthen our microbiome.
  • They help with trauma.
  • They help lower cholesterol.
  • They help keep blood pressure down.
